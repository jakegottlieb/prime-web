Carbon Monoxide Detector (CO)

The Supervised Photoelectric Carbon Monoxide detector is a UL listed photoelectric smoke alarm with a built-in transmitter designed for use with our AmberSelect receiver. When carbon monoxide (CO) is detected, the alarm sounds a loud local alarm. A sophisticated microprocessor receives data from a sensor, calculates the CO level and leak duration, and provides an alarm before dangerous CO levels are present. 

Because CO is a colorless, odorless and tasteless, yet very toxic gas that is produced when household heating and cooking fuels are not fully burnt up, quick identification of a leak is vital.  

Built into the CO detector is a loud alarm buzzer (95db) and a large flashing LED alarm. The main unit dials out and our operators notify the client and local authorities.

We recommend at least one CO detector and many of our clients have multiple detectors in the different rooms of their house.
